# TindaDone POS 🏪

TindaDone is a premium, offline-first mobile Point-of-Sale (POS) system built for sari-sari stores, mini groceries, boutiques, food stalls, and local small businesses.

Designed for speed, simplicity, and reliability, TindaDone helps business owners manage daily sales, inventory, customer credit (Utang), and expenses—all from a single mobile device.


## 🎯 Purpose

Managing a small business often means juggling multiple notebooks, receipts, and manual records for:
- Sales
- Inventory
- Customer credit (Utang)
- Daily expenses

TindaDone eliminates that complexity.

It provides a fast, professional-grade POS experience that works even without internet, helping store owners focus less on manual tracking and more on growing their business.

## 🚀 Core Features

### 🛒 Smart Selling & Checkout

Built for fast-paced daily transactions.

- Lightning-fast cart system for quick selling
